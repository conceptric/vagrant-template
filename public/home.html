What is Cyberstruction?

I build websites under my business name, Conceptric. When it comes to web hosting I could deploy the software to a third party web host and leave my client to it. Feedback from clients has shown me that they are not keen to get that deeply involved. This is where Cyberstruction was born: a fully managed web hosting service to exclusively compliment my web projects.

Benefits

The benefits to the client are apparent: they can avoid all the technical baggage of web development. I benefit from closer control of the project quality and the ability to handle development in a consistent manner, contact me if you want to know more about this.

The Future

At the moment this is not a independent business venture, I'm trialling it on an informal basis with a few key customers. If the idea catches on I may extend it into a full commercial enterprise, adopting websites developed elsewhere.
